What is a CTF?

A CTF is a competition where teams of hackers compete to solve challenges in categories such as web exploitation, reverse engineering, cryptography, and more.

CTFs are a great way to learn new skills and meet new people. TUCTF is beginner friendly, so don't be afraid to join!

What is TUCTF?

TUCTF is an annual Capture the Flag competition hosted by the CTF club at the University of Tulsa.

Our goal is to provide a fun and educational experience for all participants, regardless of skill level.

How do I play?

Register for the competition and join our Discord server. On the day of the competition, you will be given a link to the competition platform.

Once the competition starts, you will be able to view the challenges and submit flags to earn points.

What are the rules?

Teams may have up to 5 members. You may not share flags or answers with other teams. You may not attack the competition infrastructure.

For a full list of rules, see the rules page.
